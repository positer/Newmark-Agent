## Performance verdict rule

The benchmark defines a cold sample as a fresh process, Newmark root, and Electron profile; it deliberately does not claim that the Windows filesystem cache is flushed. Acceptance requires interactive P50 at most 3 seconds, P95 at most 5 seconds, maximum at most 8 seconds, Browser first-open P95 at most 2 seconds, four-times-CPU input latency P95 at most 150 ms, and no Browser guest before demand in the first five seconds. Startup-before-Browser private bytes pass at P95 at most 525 MiB or at least 25% below the configured 696 MiB baseline. Browser-on-demand acceptance is separate and requires both total private-bytes P95 at most 696 MiB and same-run incremental P95 at most 300 MiB. Equality at each limit passes.
